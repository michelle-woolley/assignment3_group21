what we have done

Similar to the way we commenced assignment 2, our group began by creating a shared word document for us to collaborate on together. Tasks were allocated based on each team member's interests and strengths. Each member individually completed their allocated sections and added them to the group word document. This information was then transferred across to our group GitHub webpage. The other main tools used by our team were MS Teams, used for communication and meetings; GitHub, for content creation and collaboration; and Figma, for wireframe and prototype designing.

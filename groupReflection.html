Timothy

As this assignment wraps up, I feel both a burden lift off of me and a welcome sense of accomplishment. I believe we have created an interesting product pitch with plenty of potential and despite many difficult roadblocks and communication issues along the way, we managed to complete every component to what I believe is to a high standard. I was pleasantly surprised over the quality of our app prototype! Part of me was sceptical about Figma before I started using it, but in the end I found that it worked excellently.

If I were to be given the chance to redo this assignment from the beginning. I would ensure far better planning for the times we have our meetings. Group

As a group, we feel that we collaborated reasonably well as a team despite facing a number of difficulties along the way. Similarly to our last assignment, we all acknowledge that we should have commenced work on the content much sooner than what we did. Despite this, we managed to come together in the final weeks to produce an assignment of a high quality that we are all happy with.

We followed a similar format for allocation of work that we did throughout assignment 2 with members completing sections based on interests and skill sets. Michelle designed the user interface prototype of the project with Timothy contributing the bulk of the information displayed in the prototype. Michelle, Timothy, Jorin, and Zorren worked together to complete the written content of the report. Lachlan created the basic outline of the website for the project. Luke was in a unique situation, currently being away on deployment with the army, and was therefore unable to contribute to this assignment.

There are a number of things we recognise as a group that we could have done better to help us avoid a lot of last minute stress to get everything completed on time. The main things we acknowledge we need to improve on are communication and time management. The lack of communication lead to delays in making progress on written elements of the project and the presentation. If there had been no extension to the submission date, we would not have met the deadline. In addition, better management of our time would have also prevented the occurrence of progress delays.

As a team, we were surprised at our ability to come together to work on a project and take it from being a small idea and begin turning it into a reality. While we were not able to make as much progress on it as we would have liked to; we are proud of the artifacts we managed to produce.

Reflecting on what we have learnt about working in a team, we all agree that effective communication is the key to success. Earlier and more regular team meetings and frequent communication would have led to us making more consistent progress as a group.
